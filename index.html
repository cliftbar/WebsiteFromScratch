What I'm doing now

Current Job

At Karen Clark and Company, we develop computer models of distasters for insurance companies, allowing them to better understand the cost of these disasters. Our transparent and open model is unique in the industry, and gives clients the tools to make their own model assumptions and risk views. As a Risk Analyst I focus on the Data Analytics side of the things, prepping the information that goes into our software. I'm involved in the whole process: finding, cleaning, validating data; analyzing and visualizing the data, and coding the tools to support those; and finally interperting the data and acting on the results.


I also help out with software development, improving existing code and experimenting with new technologies to incorperate into our product. Some of the tools I've worked with include C#/.NET, SQL, CUDA/Parallel Computing, Python, GIS, R Stats, and VBA/Excel.
